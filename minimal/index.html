About Cambodia Monitor v3.0

Cambodia Monitor v3.0 is an AI-powered intelligence dashboard focused on Cambodia. It features a 3D globe, AI news analysis, economic forecasting, strategic risk assessment, and real-time data aggregation.


AI Features: Sentiment analysis, auto-categorization, intelligence briefings, economic forecasting, strategic risk scoring

3D Globe: Globe.gl with Cambodia markers and trade route arcs

News Sources: Khmer Times, PP Post, VOA, Cambodianess, Reuters, Al Jazeera, BBC, AP, Nikkei Asia

Economic Data: World Bank API (GDP, inflation, FDI, governance indicators)

Weather: Open-Meteo API (4 cities, real-time)

Exchange Rates: 8 currency pairs updated hourly (open.er-api.com)


All APIs are free and require no keys. Built with Globe.gl, Leaflet.js, and AI-powered analytics.
