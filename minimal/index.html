About Cambodia Monitor v4.0

Cambodia Monitor v4.0 is a real-time intelligence platform focused on Cambodia, inspired by WorldMonitor.


Map: Interactive 3D globe and 2D flat map with flight routes, shipping lanes, submarine cables, and infrastructure pins.

AI Features: Sentiment analysis, categorization, intelligence briefings, economic forecasting, strategic risk scoring.

News Sources: Khmer Times, PP Post, VOA, Cambodianess, Reuters, Al Jazeera, BBC, AP, Nikkei Asia + GDELT Global.

GDELT Intelligence: Real-time global news monitoring from 100+ languages with media tone trends.

Cambodia Stock Exchange: CSX index and listed securities with daily price tracking.

Economic Data: World Bank API (GDP, inflation, FDI, governance indicators).

Weather: Open-Meteo API (4 cities, real-time).

Exchange Rates: 8 currency pairs updated hourly.


All APIs are free and require no keys.
